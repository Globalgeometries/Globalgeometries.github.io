About Me

I am Dr. Shobha Bagai, Professor and Director at the Cluster Innovation Centre, University of Delhi. With a Ph.D. in Mathematics from IIT Delhi, I specialize in Differential Equations, Fluid Dynamics, and innovative Mathematics Pedagogy aligned with NEP 2020.

My research focuses on mathematical modeling in fluid dynamics (particularly porous media) and developing innovative teaching methodologies for undergraduate mathematics education. I have supervised over 50 undergraduate research projects and mentored numerous doctoral students.
